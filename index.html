About This App
Downrange Digital is a full-featured shooting sports match scorer developed by PhantaLux Studio, supporting Solo, Head-to-Head, and Team run formats across pistol, rifle, and multi-discipline competitions.

Features include factory presets for popular shooting sports, a guided Match Queue scoring flow, full-screen Live Mode, master timer with per-shooter time stamping, optional microphone-based shot detection, and a customizable rules engine with time bonuses, miss penalties, and DNF tracking.
